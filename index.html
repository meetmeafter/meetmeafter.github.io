Meet Me After

Your legacy can live on for future generations. Meet Me After makes it happen.

HOW IT WORKS:
Never before in history have humans been able to speak to future generations like they can today. Meet Me After equips you with the tools to create your own future Legacy Page, which will let you introduce yourself to the future, share stories of your past, and offer wisdom for descendants yet to come. No longer will people wonder who you were; they will know who you are.

WHAT DO I DO?
When you enroll in Meet Me After, we will begin working with you to customize your future Legacy Page. Your Legacy Page will be uniquely you - and can include video messages, photographs, a biography, and much more. No one else knows your story like you do. Leave it behind for the world to discover. Want an example of what one Legacy Page looks like? Click here to see Frank's.

IS IT DIFFICULT?
It's easy as 1-2-3! Each registrant will be assigned a Meet Me After mentor to walk you through the process and make sure your future legacy page is exactly as you want it for future generations to see. As soon as you are finished providing your videos, photographs, and more, we'll take it from there. For more information, visit our enrollment page.

Meet Me After Timeline

1

Enrollment

When you enroll in Meet Me After, our team will get started on creating your future Legacy Page and coordinate with you on ways to fill it up. We will mail you your very own Legacy Label now, so that you can arrange for a memorial studio or family member to apply it to your future gravestone. You'll also provide us with a designated contact (memorial studio, loved one, friend) who will be responsible for contacting us to activate your Legacy Page when the time comes.

2

Creating Your Legacy

Once enrolled, we will provide you with a submission page where you will be free to submit videos messages, photographs, written biographies, family/friend testimonials, and more to us that we will add to your future legacy page. Our hope is that it will be many years until we activate your Legacy Page, so you can either submit everything once and forget about it, or come back often to add new content! Your job is to live your life to the fullest now, and we'll do the work behind-the-scenes for you. 

3

Sharing Your Legacy

Your designated contact will assume the role of notifier; informing us when you've passed on to the next chapter. Additionally, our team will do periodic research to ensure no enrollee is forgotten should a contact fail to inform us. When we are informed, your Legacy Page will go live and customized Legacy Label will be activated. It's at this point where people can experience you again, from your perspective.
